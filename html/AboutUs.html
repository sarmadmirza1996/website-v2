China Overview

Prepare for the ultimate Summer of a Lifetime and clamber aboard the tumultuous Red Dragon for a white-knuckle spectacle with Camp China.

A trip offering the opportunity to experience enlightening cultural exhilaration, your Asian adventure will unlock the gateway to a memorable experience, adorned with delectable cuisine, wondrous wildlife and newfound companions.

A beguiling burst of modernity and tradition, China’s diverse, yet riveting attractions continue to charm and enthrall countless visitors – succumb to the charisma of a true geographical gem and prepare to embrace the splendor of Asia.

Whether you’re sauntering through stunning Tianfu Square, the largest square in southwest China, navigating your way through the chic-ancient streets of Kuanzhai Alley or marveling at the monumental Leshan Giant Buddha overseeing the Sichuan landscape, the staggering Chinese spectacle is guaranteed to surpass all expectations.

The panda, an animal strongly affiliated within the Chinese community, is revered in China; the program offers applicants the chance to work with these incredible creatures, to preserve and improve their lives – yet another example of a prospective memory strong enough to break down the colossal, meandering Great Wall Of China… break down boundaries and build reminiscences to last a lifetime with Camp China.
